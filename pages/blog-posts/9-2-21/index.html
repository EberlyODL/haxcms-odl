Over the summer, our office laid the ground work to begin implementing VR into our online course spaces. Specifically, we began development of a 3D model-player that we could place inline with our course content.

Our model-player leverages Google technology to render 3D models on the web, and it's supported by all modern browsers and devices. Current Features:

  • Option to include multiple 3D Assets
  • Ability to insert detailed descriptions and images
  • Possibility to play animations
  • Choice to offer assessment opportunities

Join me in the video below and I'll take you through a quick preview of the model-player illustrating some firearm models our team has been creating for Forensic Science. Keep in mind that we're still finalzing development, so this intended only as a preview and is not the finished product.
