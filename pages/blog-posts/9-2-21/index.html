Virtual Reality (VR) is creating new opportunities to engage students with immersive content every day. The increased availability, newer technology, and lower costs have reduced the barrier to entry for many resulting in a rapidly growing community using the platform for its learning and socializing capabilities.

Over the summer, our office laid the ground work to begin implementing webVR into our online course spaces. WebVR is an open specification that makes it possible to experience VR on your browser or device.

Our model-player leverages Google web VR technology to render 3D models on modern browsers and devices. Current Features:

  • Option to include multiple 3D Assets
  • Ability to insert detailed descriptions and images
  • Possibility to play animations
  • Choice to offer assessment opportunities
