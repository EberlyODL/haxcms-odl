Recently, our office was approached by Louis Leblond to create a custom instructional element for his online course. Louis is an Associate Teaching Professor and Director of Online Education in Physics here at Eberly College of Science. Specifically, he was looking to create an attention-grabbing notification that he could easily insert throughout his course that would alert his students to relevant textbook information they should be reading.

The overall process was very simple. Louis had an idea, emailed our team with the details, and we got to work. I used the specifications that Louis requested to rapidly develop a prototype for his review.
