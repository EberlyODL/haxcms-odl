Recently (May 24th - 25th), Michael Potter and I had the opportunity to present at the Tech Pros Conference at Penn State Dickinson Law. For those unfamiliar, Tech Pros is a conference that visits a different Commonwealth Campus each year to increase development and networking opportunities for IT professionals at the university. In past years, the conference has mainly focused on classroom technology, media integration, and technical support strategies. This year, the conference introduced a new track for the web, so we jumped on the opportunity to demonstrate some of the work we have been doing in Virtual Reality (VR).
