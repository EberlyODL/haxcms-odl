Honorlock is a proctoring service that combines artificial intelligence and human proctors, creating a fair and equitable experience for students while ensuring exam integrity. It directly integrates with Canvas and allows instructors to customize college exams online, monitor performance, and generate robust analytics. Students can take their online proctored exam at any time, day or night. 24/7 support for students and faculty available via phone, live chat, and email.

How It's Used

  • Proctoring for online exams

Benefits

  • Canvas Integration
  • Works with exams in 3rd-party platforms
  • Penn State License (FREE for instructors and students)

Considerations

  • Students must use Chrome browser on a computer (no mobile devices)
  • A single practice exam must be given before any graded exams

Use and Access

  1. Activate Honorlock in your Canvas course menu (See: Add and LTI at Course Level)
  2. Once visible in your course menu, click on it
