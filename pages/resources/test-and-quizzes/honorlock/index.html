Honorlock is a proctoring service that uses artificial intelligence and human proctoring to create fair and equitable testing experiences for students. Honorlock empowers faculty to monitor exams with a number of services including access to useful analytics, cellphone detection, and 24/7 technical support via phone, live-chat, and email. Beyond that, Honorlock integrates directly with Canvas allowing both students and faculty the flexibility to access their exams easily, without sacraficing security or online integrity.

How It's Used

  • Proctoring for online exams

Benefits

  • Canvas Integration
  • Penn State License (FREE for instructors and students)
  • Cellphone detection

Considerations

  • Students must use Chrome browser on a computer (no mobile devices)
  • A single practice exam must be given before any graded exams

Use and Access

  1. Activate Honorlock in your Canvas course menu (See: Add and LTI at Course Level)
  2. Once visible in your course menu, click on it
