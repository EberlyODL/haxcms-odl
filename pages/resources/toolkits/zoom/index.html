Zoom combines cloud video conferencing, simple online meetings, group messaging, and a software-defined conference room solution into one easy-to-use platform.

With Zoom, you can:

  • Bring in an expert from a distance to share information with your class
  • Host online meetings, office hours, or recitations for people at a distance
  • Record a presentation or demonstration from your own computer
  • Share your screen to show how to solve problems, use software, or navigate a website
  • Encourage collaboration or discussion via the main meeting room or group breakout rooms
  • Archive information for people to view later at their own pace
