• I feel it's helpful to give students a welcome message so they know what to expect.
  • Tagging your posts will make sorting topics and questions later much easier.
  • Merge duplicate questions to create common discussion threads where students can collectively provide and receive feedback.
  • When a student posts a question, try not to answer it right away. This small pause will allow time for other students to provide input, which will encourage quality discussions.
  • Avoid questions like, "what's the answer to question 3?" Instead, prompt students with questions that will have them explain their answers to work and the processes they tried get them.
