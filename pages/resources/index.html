The Center for Excellence in Science Education

The Center for Excellence in Science Education (CESE) in the Eberly College of Science mission is to provide faculty and students with a collaborative educational network that promotes excellence in science teaching and learning.

CESE offers a variety of instructional development activities for faculty and students interested in science teaching.

Contingency Planning

Sometimes the unexpected happens. The University closes due to snow, illness, or some other reason. Or maybe you just can't teach. Our Contingency Planning pages are filled with loads of tips for what to expect when the unexpected occurs.
