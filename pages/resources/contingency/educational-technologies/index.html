Ed Tech Advisor

Ed Tech Advisor is a decision support tool designed to help PSU faculty pick the Penn State-supported tool that best meets their teaching needs. Ed Tech Advisor ETA enables you to match pedagogical needs to ed tech resources available at Penn State.

Pedagogical Practices

This project, Pedagogical Practices , led by Stephanie Edel-Malizia at TLT, focuses on theory and/or research-based practices that can be used in any instructional format, including residential instruction, online learning, and blended classrooms. Each pedagogical approach is described in a clear and concise format so you can quickly and easily understand how they can be used to engage students. These pedagogical approaches give instructors clear purpose for why Canvas and other technologies should be considered in order to improve student success.

WebApps

The WebApps site allows students and faculty to access applications commonly found in a Penn State Lab from the convenience of a web browser.

Courseware

The Penn State Courseware site describes what courseware is and how you access it. A broad definition of Courseware includes any digital application or software used by students or educators for a class. Courseware provides online learning tools such as lessons, homework sessions, and quizzes or tests. For Courseware to be used in a class, it must be reviewed and authorized by the University to ensure it meets the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and accessibility requirements.

Science of Learning Guide to Education Technology

The Center for Transformative Teaching & Learning at St. Andrew's Episcopal School has developed a guide called the, Science of Learning Guide to Education Technology. It was created by everyday classroom teachers in an everyday school, and designed to help teachers use education technology in the most effective way possible. It starts with the science of how students learn, lays out a simple path to get learning to happen, then maps real-world tested EdTech tools that support learning.
