When moving your assessments online, there are a few resources you may be able to use:

  • Turnitin - Turnitin is a web-based writing assessment toolkit which allows instructors to provide feedback to students through markup tools, rubrics, proofing tools and originality reports to detect plagiarism. Turnitin also provides peer review options.
  • Canvas Quiz Log - Canvas Quiz log is a tool that can be turned on for each of your Canvas that will help you identify if students are navigating away from the Canvas space during quizzing.
  • Implement many low-stakes assessments as opposed to few high stakes assessments.
  • Have students generate material, such as a paper, project, or even a video.
  • Have students work in groups
  • Have students submit hand-written assignments (so you can check for consistency across assignments)
  • Have students submit e-portfolios
