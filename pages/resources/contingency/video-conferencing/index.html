Penn State has adopted Zoom and Kaltura to work together to provide the University with an optimal conferencing and media-management service. Both tools will serve to enhance Penn State’s live-streaming and media management capabilities.

  • Zoom: Zoom unifies cloud video conferencing, simple online meetings, group messaging, and a software-defined conference room solution into one easy-to-use platform. This solution offers video, audio, and wireless screen-sharing experience across Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android, Blackberry, Zoom Rooms, and H.323/SIP room systems.

  • Kaltura: Kaltura is Penn State’s enterprise-level, cloud-based media management platform for storing, publishing, and streaming videos, video collections, and other media. Kaltura will work in tandem with Zoom, the University’s conferencing system, to help users record, store, and stream media.
