Study Habits

Things may feel out-of-control right now. You may be facing a lot of unknowns and disruptions. Try to be patient with yourself, your classmates, and your instructors during this time. Take care of your well-being ﬁrst. Making a plan and adjusting your studying may help you feel even a little sense of control.
