CAPS

CAPS is offering to all PSU students, undergraduate and graduate, a twice-daily drop-in support opportunity. The sessions are called, "Virtual Life Hacks" and will run M-F at 10:00am AND 3:30pm for one hour.

Each Life Hacks offers a 20-25 min presentation about the transitions and commonly-held feelings and stressors associated with this time of COVID and the shift to remote learning. It also offers coping strategies and small group discussions with CAPS staff facilitating them.

Study Habits

Students may be feeling a bit overwhelmed and out of control right now. They are possibly facing many unknowns and disruptions. As faculty, we want to give them practical suggestions that will help them navigate remote learning and all the things that go along with it. Most importantly, we want them to know that we care about them and their well-being. This tip sheet is a great resource to help students to plan and adjust their strategies for learning during this disruptive time.
