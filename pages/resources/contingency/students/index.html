Study Habits

Students may be feeling a bit overwhelmed and out of control right now. They are possibly facing many unknowns and disruptions. As faculty, we want to give them practical suggestions that will help them navigate remote learning and all the things that go along with it. Most importantly, we want them to know that we care about them and their well-being. This tip sheet is a great resource to help students to plan and adjust their strategies for learning during this disruptive time.
